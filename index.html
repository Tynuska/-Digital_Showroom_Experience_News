Digital Showroom Experience News
March 2023 Edition
 
Dear Business Partners,
This is the year’s first Digital Showroom Experience News. The most noteworthy news is the release of DISE 4.0, which introduces the changes described below. Download the new version of the app now to offer customers in your showrooms the latest content and an even better showroom experience.
Here’s wishing you a year brimming with innovations
The Digital Showroom Experience Team
 
New Škoda identity for Sales Support Tool and touch tools
In conjunction with the launch of our new corporate identity, we are also rolling out a new design for our digital tools. The SST app and touch tools are the first to be redesigned.
 
New models and product update
The DISE apps now feature content for the Enyaq iV RS, Enyaq Coupé iV and Enyaq Coupé iV Sportline (Car Visualiser, Reasons to Buy articles and technical data on engines). What’s more, the product content has been completely updated in line with the model care from the end of last year.
 
New Disclaimer function
A Disclaimer function is now available in the administration of the Device App, Sales Support Tool and Car Information Stand. It can be easily turned on and off in CMS jNP. Obviously, each market is free to include a disclaimer of its own wording that reflects the legislation applicable to that market. The Disclaimer is displayed on the frontend of all devices.
 
New website address for managing cars for CIS
With the new data structure, the web address for creating and editing cars for Car Information Stand (CIS) has changed. Now you can manage cars for Car Information Stand (CIS) at diseadmin.skoda-auto.com/sms.
 
System enhancements and stabilisation of DISE apps
All DISE apps have been given extensive system enhancements. The optimisation of the data update process has made the apps more stabilised and improved the customer experience.
 
Automatic data update for the dealer network
In the Sales Support Tool, you now have the option to set up automatic data updates for the dealer network. This function lets you automatically update content in Škoda Tube, Car Visualiser, accessories, technical specifications, and articles. The feature is available for the touch tools and Video Wall.
 
Importer's next steps
Update your CMS jNP as per your market conditions and your sales programmes (configuration of visualisation, model structure and competitors).
Localise the updated content for your market. Request source files with new items for translation at digital.showroom@skoda-auto.cz.
Inform your dealer network about news.
Dealer's next steps
Download the latest version of the SST app – version 4.0.
This updates the data of all applications via the Sales Support Tool. Either manually or automatically (default option). Automatic updates can be disabled in the SST settings.
Restart all devices within the digital showroom (not relevant for SST only markets).
Download the updated content for all available devices within the showroom.
